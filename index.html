My Projects

Environmental Awareness

Since October 2024, I have been working as an environment ambassador in collaboration with VolunteerKRD and HASAR Organisation. This project aims to raise awareness about environmental issues, including climate change, waste management, and sustainable practices. Through workshops, campaigns, and community engagement, we strive to educate the public and inspire collective action towards a greener future.

Kurdistan Space Research Agency (KSRA)

The Kurdistan Space Research Agency (KSRA) aims to push the boundaries of space research and technological advancements within the Kurdistan region. Through strategic partnerships, we focus on educational outreach, space exploration, and the development of critical technologies to support innovation.

SoraniSync

Launched in February 2022, SoraniSync is an individual project dedicated to translating online applications into the Sorani Kurdish language. This initiative helps break language barriers and provides Kurdish speakers with better access to digital tools and resources, enhancing inclusivity in the digital space.

Mission

KSRA is dedicated to advancing space research and technology in Kurdistan. We aim to inspire young minds and lead the way in space science, creating a sustainable future for Kurdistan in space exploration.

Projects

KSRA is working on a variety of exciting space projects, including the development of CanSat kits, space research partnerships, and educational initiatives.

  • CanSat Project: Developing miniature satellites for educational purposes
  • PocketQube Project: Advancing mini satellite technologies
  • Kurdistan Space Exploration Initiative: Building the first space observatory
